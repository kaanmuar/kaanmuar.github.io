A Senior IT Project Manager and QA Architect with over two decades of experience delivering high-quality, complex software solutions for global technology leaders. Expert in strategic planning, stakeholder management, and leading cross-functional teams to meet project goals. Proven ability to architect and implement robust QA automation frameworks (Selenium, Cypress, Kotlin), ensuring exceptional product quality and efficiency for clients like Autodesk, AT&T, and Sophos.

Throughout my career, I have championed a holistic approach to quality, embedding QA processes into every stage of the SDLC. As a QA Architect, I specialize in designing scalable, maintainable, and efficient automation frameworks from the ground up, tailored to the specific needs of the product and technology stack. My expertise extends to selecting the optimal tools, establishing best practices, and creating CI/CD integrations that provide rapid, reliable feedback to development teams, significantly reducing regression cycles and accelerating time-to-market.

As a Project Manager and Leader, I excel at building and mentoring high-performing, cross-cultural teams. I have a proven track record of managing multi-million dollar project portfolios, mitigating risks, and aligning technical execution with executive-level business strategy. My leadership philosophy is centered on clear communication, empowerment, and fostering a collaborative environment where every team member is motivated to contribute their best work. My C2 English proficiency and multilingual abilities allow me to seamlessly manage global teams and stakeholders.
